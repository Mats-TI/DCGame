What is this game?

The "DC:Into The Universe Quiz Game, Justice league Trivia Test" is a quiz game that tests basic knowledge about prominent Detective Comics characters. It focuses on the Pre-New 52 and Post Crisis of Infinite Earths Justice League. The members of the Justic League included are Superman, Batman, Wonder Woman, The Flash, Hawkgirl, Martian Manhunter and Green Lantern.

How to play

To Play the game, select your superhero and answer questions pertaining to them, choosing your answers from a list of predetermined options. There are five questions in total per superhero and the object of the game is to score the highest amount of points per superhero. Good luck and have fun
